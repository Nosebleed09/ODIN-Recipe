Paneer Tikka

Description

Paneer Tikka is a popular Indian appetizer made by marinating paneer cubes in a spiced yogurt mixture. The marinated paneer, along with vegetables, is then grilled or baked until charred and flavorful. It's typically served with mint chutney and a squeeze of lemon juice.

Ingredients for the dish

  • 200 grams paneer (cubed)
  • 1/4 cup yogurt (thick)
  • 1 tablespoon gram flour (besan)
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon chaat masala
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 small onion, cut into squares
  • 1 small bell pepper, cut into squares
  • Skewers (wooden or metal)

Lets cook it !

  1. Prepare the marinade: In a bowl, mix yogurt, gram flour, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, red chili powder, garam masala, chaat masala, lemon juice, salt, and oil.
  2. Add paneer cubes: Gently coat the paneer cubes in the marinade. Add the onion and bell pepper pieces. Let it marinate for 30 minutes.
  3. Skewer the paneer and veggies: Thread the marinated paneer, onion, and bell pepper alternately onto skewers.
  4. Cook:
    • Grill method: Preheat the grill. Place the skewers on the grill and cook for 5-7 minutes on each side until the paneer is lightly charred.
    • Oven method: Preheat the oven to 200°C (390°F). Place the skewers on a baking tray and bake for 15-20 minutes, turning halfway through.
    • Stovetop method: Heat a pan with a little oil and cook the skewers on medium heat, turning them to cook evenly on all sides.
  5. Serve: Squeeze some lemon juice over the Paneer Tikka and serve hot with mint chutney.
