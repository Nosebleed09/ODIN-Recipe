Daal Khichdi

Description

Dal Khichdi is a comforting one-pot dish combining rice and moong dal (lentils) with vegetables and spices. Cooked in a pressure cooker, it's known for its soft and mushy texture, making it a nutritious and easy-to-digest meal. It’s often enjoyed with yogurt or pickle.

Ingredients for the dish

  • 1/2 cup rice
  • 1/2 cup split yellow moong dal
  • 1 tablespoon ghee (clarified butter) or oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon asafoetida (hing)
  • 1/2 teaspoon ginger paste (optional)
  • 1/4 cup chopped vegetables (like carrots, peas, potatoes) – optional
  • 4 cups water
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish (optional)

Lets cook it !

  1. Wash and soak the rice and moong dal together for 15-20 minutes.
  2. Heat ghee in a pressure cooker. Add cumin seeds, and let them splutter.
  3. Add asafoetida, turmeric powder, and ginger paste. Sauté for a few seconds.
  4. Add the soaked rice and dal (drain the water) and sauté for 2 minutes.
  5. Add chopped vegetables if using, and sauté for another 2 minutes.
  6. Pour in 4 cups of water and add salt. Stir well.
  7. Pressure cook for 3-4 whistles until the rice and dal are soft and mushy.
  8. Let the pressure release naturally, then open the lid and stir the khichdi.
  9. Garnish with fresh cilantro if desired. Serve hot with yogurt or pickle.
