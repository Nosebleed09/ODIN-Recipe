Sweet Lime soda

Description

Sweet Lime Soda is a refreshing beverage made from fresh sweet lime juice mixed with sugar, black salt, and chilled soda water. It's often garnished with mint leaves and served over ice, making it a perfect thirst-quencher for warm days.

Ingredients for the dish

  • 1 sweet lime (mosambi), juiced
  • 1 tablespoon sugar (adjust to taste)
  • A pinch of black salt (kala namak)
  • A pinch of regular salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon chaat masala (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon lemon juice (optional, for extra tanginess)
  • 1 cup chilled soda water
  • Ice cubes
  • Fresh mint leaves for garnish (optional)

Lets cook it !

  1. Prepare the juice: Extract the juice from the sweet lime and strain it to remove any seeds or pulp.
  2. Mix the ingredients: In a glass, add the sweet lime juice, sugar, black salt, regular salt, and chaat masala (if using). Stir well until the sugar dissolves completely.
  3. Add soda: Pour in the chilled soda water and stir gently to mix.
  4. Add ice and garnish: Add a few ice cubes to the glass. Garnish with fresh mint leaves if desired.
  5. Serve immediately: Enjoy your refreshing Sweet Lime Soda!
